Meet Dr. Nivedha

M.D Paediatrics, ACLP (Advanced Certified Lactation Professional)

I provide compassionate, evidence-based care for infants, children, and adolescents with a special focus on early-life nutrition and breastfeeding support. My practice includes the management of common childhood illnesses, growth and developmental concerns, newborn care, and adolescent health.


I am particularly passionate about lactation care, helping mothers and families navigate breastfeeding challenges such as latching difficulties, low milk supply, pain, and feeding concerns. I offer practical, personalized guidance to support successful and comfortable breastfeeding.

Core Services

Paediatric & Newborn Care

Comprehensive management of childhood illnesses, growth monitoring, and developmental concerns.

Lactation Support

Establishing pain-free latching, maintaining milk supply, and addressing feeding concerns.

Nutritional Guidance

Expert breastfeeding counseling and complementary feeding guidance for your growing baby.
